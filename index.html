Our Mandate

F20F works to transform lives through the structural reengineering of the human will. Our model joins youth potential, intergenerational discipline, ethical formation, and institutional rigor to create durable social change.

"We form principled leaders, free from addictions, resistant to manipulation, capable of serving truth, defending dignity, and changing society from within."
The Methodology

The Factor 20 System

Factor 20 is not motivational language and not a superficial routine. It is a structured system for inner strengthening, ethical programming, and permanent transformation of the will.


The visible result appears on Day 21, but the real revolution occurs during the previous 20 days: the hidden stage where the will hardens, identity stabilizes, and transformation becomes irreversible.

Science of Wanting to Be

Treaty of the 20 Keys

A sequence of ethical codes designed to move the person from vulnerability to conscious self-mastery through four transformational stages.

I
Days 1–5

Foundation — Unlocking the Self

The first stage confronts weakness, noise, and fragmentation. The goal is inner order, moral opening, and the beginning of structural self-command.

Faith Firmness Fortitude Strength Fearlessness
II
Days 6–10

Ignition — Forging Character

Discipline becomes active. Identity begins to hold form under pressure, and the person learns to sustain commitment instead of depending on emotion.

Fervor Focus Fire Fidelity Favor
III
Days 11–15

Social Structure — Ethical Expansion

The transformed will begins to project outward. Leadership, cooperation, justice, and social conscience become practical responsibilities.

Fairness Freedom Friendship Prosperity Future
IV
Days 16–20

Consolidation — Final Transcendence

The will is consolidated into identity. The person is no longer reacting to life, but governing it through awareness, responsibility, ethics, and direction.

Moving Forward Responsibility Awareness Ethics Transformation
21

Day 21: The Permanent Mutation. What the world sees as an external result is actually the visible expression of an internal process already completed. Factor 20 does not create a habit. It forms a new structure of will.

The Flaca Effect
Mystique of Origin

Bolivia — Our Emotional Heart

The emotional origin of F20F is Bolivia. There, a dog found at the edge of destruction was rescued, treated, reconstructed, and loved back into life. Her name was Flaca.


Flaca became the living proof that love, when executed with discipline, method, and continuity, can become real transformational technology. What happened to her is the symbolic foundation of what F20F seeks to do for human beings and vulnerable communities.

Programs & Initiatives

Strategic Lines of Action

Six pillars of intervention connecting animal welfare, youth leadership, truth, resilience, culture, and food sovereignty.

Animal Rescue & Transformation

Flaca Koin and institutional funding support surgeries, sterilization campaigns, and rescue interventions for abandoned animals in Bolivia.

Positive Leadership & 0 Alcohol

We establish sobriety, self-command, and moral discipline as the biological and spiritual base of authentic leadership.

Women as Agents of Change

Women are empowered as leaders in resilience, care systems, crisis response, and transformational social intelligence.

Media Truth & Democracy

We train citizens and young leaders to identify manipulation, reject fake narratives, and defend democratic truth.

Food Sovereignty — Hydroponics

Urban food production, resilience training, and practical sustainability reconnect communities with disciplined self-sufficiency.

Cultural Work — Nature & Art

We reconnect youth with art, nature, memory, and great human legacies to resist digital emptiness and recover depth.
